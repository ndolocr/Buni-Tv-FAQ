General

Buni.tv is the leading video-on-demand service for the best movies, TV series and kids content from across Africa and the diaspora.

Business Opportunities

I am a filmmaker/producer/artist. Who can I contact regarding opportunities to distribute my content on Buni.tv?

Our mission is to promote creativity - even from the most remote corners of the world - and we welcome your submissions of full-length content for licensing purposes. Send a synopsis of your work and a link to a clip or trailer to content@buni.tv.

How do I make money?

Buni.tv shares all its revenue with content owners such as producers and filmmakers on a 50/50 basis. Traffic to each video is tracked on a monthly basis and revenue is attributed to each video based on the percentage of total minutes watched on that video. For example if the total minutes watched on Buni.tv generated $1,000 in revenue and your video accounts for 10% of that traffic, 10% of $1,000, or $100, will be split equally between you and Buni.tv.

How do I become a Buni.tv affiliate?

In 2015 we launched a Buni.tv affiliate program that allows bloggers to embed the Buni.tv player and earn up to 10% commision on sales made through the website. To sign up as a Buni.tv affiliate you need to have a Buni.tv account. Then sign up here http://buni.tv/affiliates and your account will be approved in 24 hours. Once your account is approved, you can embed any Buni+ videos to your website by clicking the embed link below each premium video. If you have any questions email support@buni.tv.

The video is not playing, what can I do?

Wait for the browser to completely load and try again. If you are using Opera Mini on your mobile, close this window and open your device’s native web browser e.g. Chrome, Firefox instead.

I get an error message, what can I do?

If the message says “Server not found: rtmpte://fms.413c.edgecastcdn.net:80/00413/content/”, this is likely to be an issue linked to your internet service provider. Try playing using Internet Explorer.

It’s still not working/ My problem is different, what can I do?

If problem persists do not hesitate to send us an email at support@buni.tv with your ISP, browser and device details and we’ll respond as fast as possible.

Streaming the videos is expensive, what is the cheapest way to watch Buni.tv?

We advise using WiFi either at home, office or where there's a WiFi hotspot. If there's no WiFi, we advise that you purchase unlimited internet bundles from your mobile or internet operator. This is the most cost-effective way to enjoy videos on Buni.tv.
